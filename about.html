About Us

"A goal without a plan is just a wish"

Planning a social or corporate event can be stressful if you have to be both the host and the planner. The key to a successful event is to have someone there to handle all of the production aspects so that you can enjoy your guests or be a host to your VIPs. We’re able to plan any type of event, whether it’s an anniversary, birthday, reunion, sales incentive, holiday celebration or just an old-fashioned party.


We’re more than just a boutique event-planning firm. We’re a global team with the creative capacity to design and execute an unforgettable party — whether a destination wedding, a corporate gig or a big-bash birthday. We believe in creating unique moments that inspire.
