Full Service Wedding Planning + Design

Congratulations! You’re engaged! If you’re wondering what comes after “yes!” then Full Service Wedding Planning is a good place for you to start. Whether your wedding is a one-day affair or a weekend of events, we can help! While we specialize in tented weddings, raw spaces, and private homes, we also often work in established wedding venues. Full Service Planning is designed so that we do the work and you do the fun parts! We handle everything from start to finish, taking care to ensure that your wedding is not only beautiful, but that it reflects who the two of you are - your love story.

Destination Wedding Planning + Design

Destination Weddings come with their own unique set of challenges. Our Destination Planning Service is also a Full Planning Service, but takes into account the more robust needs of a destination wedding. Whether you want to exclusively buyout a property for the weekend, provide travel assistance for your guests, or workout a ferry schedule to a remote location, our experienced team will handle the details. Contact us for a complimentary consultation for your destination wedding.

Partial Wedding Planning + Month-Of Coordination

We offer Partial Wedding Planning for couples who already have their venue and the majority of their vendors, but need help bringing their vision to life. Partial Planning design work includes color palettes and mood boards, as well as stationery and floral vendors and rentals. We also give you a little bit of homework via a design survey, so that we can put together oodles of creative ideas for your wedding day. We’ll source your favorite ideas and pull everything together on the day of. Additionally, Partial Planning includes Month-Of Coordination beginning six weeks before your wedding day.

Beginning in 2016, our exclusive Month-Of Coordination service is available only for select weddings. Please contact us to learn more about our availability. Dates are limited.

*Partial Planning is not available for tented/raw space weddings.
